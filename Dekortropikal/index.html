Need Help?

Find answers to the most common questions below. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

What materials are your artificial plants made from? +

Our artificial plants are made from high-quality materials such as silk, plastic, and polyurethane, which are designed to look as realistic as possible while being durable and low-maintenance.

Are your artificial plants safe for pets and children? +

Yes, our artificial plants are safe for both pets and children. They do not contain any toxic materials, but as with any decorative item, we recommend supervision to avoid any accidents.

Can I use artificial plants outdoors? +

Some of our artificial plants are designed for indoor use only, while others are suitable for outdoor use. Look for products labeled as "UV-resistant" for outdoor applications. Always check the product description for specific guidelines.

How do I care for my artificial plants? +

Artificial plants require minimal care. Simply dust them regularly using a soft cloth or brush. For deeper cleaning, wipe with a damp cloth. Keep them out of direct sunlight to avoid fading.

Do your plants come in different sizes? +

Yes, we offer a wide variety of artificial plants in different sizes, from small desk plants to large floor-standing trees. Please check the product specifications to find the perfect size for your space.

Are your artificial plants customizable? +

While we do not offer custom designs for individual plants, we do offer a wide range of plants that can be paired and arranged in many ways. If you have a specific need, feel free to contact us, and we will do our best to help.

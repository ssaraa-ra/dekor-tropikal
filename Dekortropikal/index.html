FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Need Help?

Find answers to the most common questions below. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

What materials are your artificial plants made from? +

Our artificial plants are made from premium silk, plastic, and polyurethane materials that closely mimic the look and texture of real plants while being long-lasting and low-maintenance.

Do you offer decoration services for events or offices? +

Yes, we provide professional decoration services for events, offices, and commercial spaces. Our team can design and install artificial plant arrangements tailored to your needs.

Are your artificial plants safe for pets and children? +

Yes, our products are non-toxic and safe for pets and children. We recommend placing them in safe spots to avoid any accidental tipping or chewing by pets.

Can artificial plants be used outdoors? +

We offer outdoor-friendly artificial plants that are UV-resistant and weatherproof. Please check the product description to ensure it’s suitable for outdoor use.

Do you provide custom plant arrangements? +

Yes, we can create custom plant arrangements for homes, offices, and events. Contact our team with your design preferences, and we’ll bring your vision to life.

How do I clean and maintain artificial plants? +

Simply dust your artificial plants regularly with a soft cloth or feather duster. For deeper cleaning, use a damp cloth or gentle soap solution. Avoid harsh chemicals to prevent damage.

Can I rent artificial plants for events? +

Yes, we offer artificial plant rentals for weddings, parties, corporate events, and exhibitions. Our rental service includes delivery, setup, and collection after your event.

Do your artificial plants look realistic? +

Absolutely! Our plants are designed with fine details, natural textures, and vibrant colors to make them look as close to real plants as possible — many clients say they can’t tell the difference.
